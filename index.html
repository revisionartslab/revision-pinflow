Privacy Policy

REVISION INSTAFLOW (the "App") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with appropriate regulations.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The App accesses the following data through the Instagram Graph API:

  • Your Instagram Business/Creator account information (ID, username, profile picture).
  • Access Tokens required for uploading media content to your specific account.

This data is used solely for the purpose of uploading content as requested by you and displaying your account's connection status within the App's user interface.

2. Data Storage and Security

  • We never ask for or store your Instagram password.
  • Authentication tokens and user data are stored locally within your browser's local storage only. They are not stored permanently on any of our servers, nor are they transmitted to third parties except for the primary purpose of interacting with the Instagram API.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties for marketing or any other purposes.

User Data Deletion Instructions (Self-Service)

Since the App does not store your personal data on any server, all data processing ceases immediately upon disconnecting the App. If you would like to revoke access or delete the connection to REVISION INSTAFLOW manually, please follow these steps:

  • Log in to your Facebook account.
  • Go to Settings & Privacy > Settings > Apps and Websites.
  • Find 'REVISION INSTAFLOW' in the list.
  • Click the 'Remove' button to revoke all App permissions.

Once you complete these steps, the App will no longer have access to your data, and your active local session will be invalidated immediately.

Data Deletion Request

If you wish to request the deletion of any data associated with your use of REVISION INSTAFLOW, please follow the formal request process below:

  • Send an email to revisionartslab@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  • Include your Instagram username associated with the App for identification purposes.

We will process your request within 48 hours. Please note that since we do not store your media or logs on our servers, "deletion" primarily involves ensuring any temporary session data is cleared.
